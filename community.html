FAQ

Questions about joining

Who can join the EAGLOPEN community?

Anyone with a genuine interest in science and technology — students, professionals, educators, and supporters. There are roles for every background and experience level, from complete beginners to senior researchers.

Does membership cost anything?

Community membership is free. Our programs are made possible by volunteers and supporters, and we work hard to keep learning accessible to every student regardless of financial background.

Can I volunteer if I am not based in Ethiopia?

Yes. Many contributions happen remotely — mentorship, code review, curriculum development, translation, and infrastructure support can all be done from anywhere in the world.

How can my organization support EAGLOPEN?

Organizations can sponsor programs, donate equipment, provide cloud or hosting resources, offer internships to our students, or partner on research. Reach out through our contact page and we will find the right fit together.

How do I get my profile featured on this page?

Contribute! Members who volunteer, mentor, teach, or support the organization are recognized here. Once you are active in the community, our coordinators will reach out to feature your profile.
