Who We Are

A community of learners, builders, and innovators working toward a brighter Africa.

EAGLOPEN was founded at Adama Science and Technology University by students who believed that learning should go beyond the classroom. Our mission is to help young people explore science, technology, research, and innovation through practical experience and meaningful collaboration

Today, EAGLOPEN brings together people who are passionate about programming, engineering, aerospace, artificial intelligence, research, entrepreneurship, and creative problem solving. Through programs like AlgoLab, collaborative projects, workshops, and community initiatives, we create opportunities for students to learn, build, and grow together.

Through years of consistent effort, mentorship, and collaboration, EAGLOPEN has built a platform where ambition meets opportunity — enabling students to go beyond traditional academics and engage deeply with programming, algorithms, artificial intelligence, and emerging technologies.

We believe that the future of Africa will be shaped by young people who are curious enough to ask questions, determined enough to solve problems, and courageous enough to build new ideas.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To become one of Africa's leading science and technology organizations, cultivating globally competitive innovators who drive meaningful change across industries and societies.

Our Mission

To deliver elite, accessible, and impact-driven education in science and technology by identifying talent early, fostering excellence, and building strong partnerships with academic and technological institutions.

Leadership

A Message from the Founder

Lencho Taye

Founder & Executive Director

EAGLOPEN Science and Technology Organization

EAGLOPEN was founded on the belief that talent exists everywhere, but opportunity does not. Our goal has always been to close that gap by creating environments where curiosity, discipline, and ambition are transformed into real-world impact.

Through initiatives like EAGLOPEN AlgoLab, we are not only teaching programming and algorithms — we are shaping mindsets, building confidence, and preparing the next generation of African innovators to compete and lead globally.

We believe in the power of education to transform lives and communities. By investing in students today, we are building the foundation for Africa's technological leadership in the future.

Lencho Taye Founder & Executive Director

Our Journey

From Initiative to Institution

  1. The Beginning

    Founded as a School-Based Initiative

    EAGLOPEN began with a simple conviction — that exceptional Ethiopian talent deserved a serious platform for science and technology.

  2. Building Momentum

    Two Years of Structured Tech Education

    Consistent, structured training for student communities laid the foundation for everything that followed.

  3. National Recognition

    First Place & Partnership with ASTU

    National recognition — including first place at the National Science and Engineering Competition — and a formal partnership with Adama Science and Technology University.

  4. Today & Beyond

    Launch of EAGLOPEN AlgoLab

    An elite program for national talent — training the next generation of African innovators in algorithms, programming, and artificial intelligence.
