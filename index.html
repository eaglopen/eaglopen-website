EAGLOPEN AlgoLab

EAGLOPEN AlgoLab is a highly selective national program conducted in partnership with ASTU. Fewer than 35 exceptional students across Ethiopia receive world-class training in programming, algorithms, and advanced computational thinking — the same rigor found at top global institutions.

  • Fewer than 35 students selected nationally
  • Conducted in partnership with ASTU
  • World-class algorithmic & AI curriculum
