The Program

What Is EAGLOPEN AlgoLab?

EAGLOPEN AlgoLab is an intensive, high-impact educational program designed to identify and train Ethiopia's most talented students in programming and algorithmic thinking. The program is built for students who demonstrate exceptional curiosity, discipline, and problem-solving ability.

Conducted within the Adama Science and Technology University (ASTU) environment, AlgoLab provides participants with university-level exposure, rigorous instruction, and mentorship from experienced lecturers and professionals.

  • University-level instruction and mentorship
  • Rigorous, competition-grade curriculum
  • A national community of exceptional peers

The Learning Journey

Program Structure

A four-phase progression that takes students from strong foundations to advanced, globally competitive problem solving.

Phase 01

Core Programming

Strong foundations in programming languages, logic, and structured problem solving.

Phase 02

Algorithms & Data Structures

Deep understanding of algorithms, complexity analysis, and efficient data handling.

Phase 03

Advanced Problem Solving

Competitive programming, logical reasoning, and real-world algorithmic challenges.

Phase 04

University Exposure

Learning within ASTU's academic environment to prepare students for higher education and research culture.

SELECTIVE ADMISSION

Admission & Selection Process

AlgoLab welcomes motivated students who are eager to learn, solve problems, and grow through programming. Each cohort is carefully selected to create a collaborative learning environment where every participant receives meaningful guidance and support.

  • Limited cohort for personalized learning
  • Passion for programming and problem solving
  • Curiosity, commitment, and willingness to learn
  • Positive attitude and collaborative mindset
