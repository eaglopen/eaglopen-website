Where EAGLOPEN's work becomes a public record

This page is the official media center of the EAGLOPEN Science and Technology Organization. It is where we document the organization's journey — the workshops we run, the research we demonstrate, the competitions we enter, and the milestones we reach along the way.

Everything here is curated for journalists, partners, students, and community members who want to see the work behind the mission. The archive grows with every program, every demonstration, and every announcement.

Media FAQ

Common questions from the press

How do I request an interview with EAGLOPEN?

Email eaglopen@proton.me or use the contact form with your outlet, topic, and deadline. The media desk will coordinate the right spokesperson.

Can I use photographs and videos from this page?

Media on this page may be used for editorial coverage of EAGLOPEN with attribution. For commercial or promotional use, please request permission through the media desk first.

Where can I find the official logo and brand assets?

The Media Resources section above hosts the logo pack, brand guidelines, and press kit as each becomes available.

How can I be notified about upcoming events?

Announcements are published in the News & Announcements section and on the News page. You can also reach out via the contact page to join our updates list.

Who do I contact to verify facts about the organization?

All facts, figures, and claims should be verified directly with the media desk at eaglopen@proton.me. We respond to verification requests as a priority.
